Our Core Values

Join our team to build next-gen institutional custody platform

Learning and growing

We believe the best outcomes for both you, PolySign and the world come through personal growth. In order to solve really hard problems,you'll need to learn and grow. You'll have accesss to an unlimited book budget, dedicated time to read, and wonderful mentors who are luminaries in our space - David Schwartz and Arthur Britto.

Support and safety

Building great things requires putting yourself out there, which is hard. We support each other no mattter what. If you're excited about something, whether it's work related or not, please share! Support and safety are how we enable each other to build great things.

Autonomy and freedom

At PolySign, every team member is trusted to do their work and not how to do it. Case in point: our engineering team works from home twice a week. We wnat you to build and run with your ideas!

Responsibility and impact

We're not maintaining somebody else's old stack here. Many technical decisions have not been made yet, and you'll get tons of say in what tools you use how you use them.
